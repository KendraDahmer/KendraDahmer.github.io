Biography

I am a postdoctoral scholar in the Pickering Lab at UC Berkeley. I am currently working to improve surveillance strategies for soil-transmitted helminths (STH) in environmental reservoirs in Benin and India. I received my PhD from the University of Wisconsin-Madison where I studied vector-borne helminths like Brugia malayi and Mansonella spp. I received my BS in Microbiology from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee.
