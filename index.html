Biography

I am a postdoctoral scholar in the Pickering lab at the University of California-Berkeley. I am currently working to improve surveillance strategies for soil-transmitted helminths in environmental reservoirs in Benin & India. I recieved my PhD from University of Wisconsin-Madison where I studied vector-borne helminths like Brugia malayi and Mansonella spp. I received my BS in microbiology from University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee.
